My journey playing lacrosse began since 2017.
>
It picked up when I became part of the Hong Kong U19 Women's Lacrosse Team,
and went to international tournaments, competing at the U19 Women's Lacrosse World Championships in 2019 in Canada.
After that, I kept up training and went on to join the Hong Kong Women's Lacrosse Team,
pushing me to develop my teamwork, time management skills, commitment, and ability to perform under pressure.
In 2022, I started my Masters at TU Delft and joined the Delft Lacrosse Diamonds.
I also had a great opportunity to train with the Women's Dutch National Team, playing for the Team B at various tournaments.
In 2023, I went on to serve as part of the Board for Delft Lacrosse.
Apart from organising matches and events, I got to apply my design skills for the association.
